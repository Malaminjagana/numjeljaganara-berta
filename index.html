Welcome to Numuyel Jaganara

We are a family connected by a shared sense of responsibility and community. Each year, contributions are made as follows:

  • Individuals residing in European countries or the United States contribute 5,000 Dalasi.
  • Those residing in The Gambia contribute 1,500 Dalasi if financially capable; otherwise, they contribute 1,000 Dalasi.
  • Individuals who are unmarried contribute 500 Dalasi.
    • These funds are utilized to cover essential household expenses in our village, including electricity, water, and general maintenance.

To ensure fairness and accountability, a fine is applied for non-compliance with these contributions:

  • European residents: ‚Ç¨10.
  • United States residents: $10.
  • Residents in The Gambia: 500 Dalasi.
We appreciate everyone's cooperation and commitment to fostering the well-being of our community.
